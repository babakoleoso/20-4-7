7-Day Practice Module

Sources of Ire / Ibi

This 7-day Iyawo module trains disciplined submission to the divination process through repeated casting, elderization, interpretation, and documentation.

The practice flow follows the Lesson 07 assignment: first establish Odu in Ire or Ibi, cast again to determine whether Ebo is needed, elderize the second cast, document the dominant Odu, and repeat the process daily.

Daily 20-4 Workflow

Each day contains six complete cycles. Every cycle follows the same movement from first cast to final documentation.

1

First Cast

Cast the elements once to establish the Odu in either Ire or Ibi.

2

Second Cast

Cast again to determine whether Ebo is needed in support of the revealed condition.

3

Transform

Transpose the result through the Basic Four Cowrie Shell Divination logic: Yes, No, or Definitely.

4

Elderize

Use elderization to reveal the source field of Ire or Ibi through Eldest, Elder, Senior, and Junior.

5

Document

Record the dominant Odu, source, support, and whether Ebo is required or not required.

6

Repeat Daily

Complete six full cycles each day for seven consecutive days and submit the work daily.

7-Day Module Layout

The process remains constant each day, but the emphasis deepens from orientation to mastery.

1

Day 1 — Orientation & Alignment

Focus: Learning the flow

Move carefully through all six cycles. Concentrate on correct casting, identifying Ire versus Ibi, and building comfort with the full order of operations.

Perform 6 complete cycles
Identify Odu as Ire or Ibi
Begin daily documentation habit
Outcome: Familiarity with the full process.
2

Day 2 — Accuracy in Casting

Focus: Precision

Strengthen accuracy in shell counting and response classification. Confirm each reading carefully before proceeding to interpretation.

Perform 6 complete cycles
Track open-shell count carefully
Match response to Yes / No / Definitely
Outcome: Greater consistency in casting.
3

Day 3 — Understanding Ebo

Focus: Decision-making

Pay close attention to when the second cast requires Ebo and when it does not. Learn to distinguish maintenance from active spiritual response.

Perform 6 complete cycles
Record every Ebo / No Ebo outcome
Note strong “Definitely” patterns
Outcome: Clearer understanding of spiritual support requirements.
4

Day 4 — Elderization Mastery

Focus: Sources of Ire / Ibi

Work more deeply with the source structure of the cast: Eldest, Elder, Senior, and Junior. Identify the field through which the energy is expressing.

Eldest → Aiku / Iku
Elder → Aje / Ofo
Senior → Rin / Ija
Junior → Omo / Oran
Outcome: Ability to identify where the condition is rooted.
5

Day 5 — Pattern Recognition

Focus: Dominant trends

Look across the six daily cycles for repetition. Observe whether certain source fields, Odu, or support patterns recur more than others.

Compare all 6 cycles side by side
Highlight repeating Odu or source fields
Notice recurrent Ebo needs
Outcome: Recognition of dominant energetic trends.
6

Day 6 — Integration

Focus: Full interpretation

Bring the entire process together. Read Odu, determine support, identify source, and describe the relationship between the dominant and supporting patterns.

Interpret each cycle as a complete unit
Connect source with support Odu
Refine written summaries
Outcome: More mature and holistic divination interpretation.
7

Day 7 — Discipline & Completion

Focus: Mastery through repetition

Complete the final six cycles with care, confidence, and clean records. Review the week as a whole and prepare the full submission.

Finish final 6 cycles
Review all 7 days of logs
Confirm all Ebos and dominant patterns are documented
Outcome: Demonstrated discipline, order, and completion.
